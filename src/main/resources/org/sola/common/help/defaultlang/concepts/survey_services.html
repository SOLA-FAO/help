Survey Services

Survey Services can be used to capture details from new plans and update the cadastral network information recorded in Registry.

Service Name

Description

Documents Reqd.

Survey

Change to Cadastre

 

Used to capture the details from new plans (i.e. Survey, Complied, Registry, etc.) that require changes to the cadastral network such as the creation of new parcels by subdivision.

Cadastral Survey

Survey

Redefine Cadastre

Used to make changes to the cadastral network outside of the receipt of a survey plan such as correcting the topology of existing parcels by adding new nodes or redefining boundaries. Note that a compiled plan or sketch should be provided to describe the changes made for the service.
